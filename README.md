# Pokémon Auto Battler

A auto-battler game built in Unity featuring Pokémon from Generations I–III. Build your team in the shop phase, watch them fight automatically in the battle phase, and climb through Gym Leaders, the Elite Four, and the Champion.

## Gameplay

**Shop Phase**
- A pool of Pokémon is offered each round
- Buy, sell, or drag-and-drop Pokémon into your team slots
- Pokémon can evolve
- Hover over any Pokémon to preview its ability, stats, type, and full evolution line

**Battle Phase**
- Your team of 6 fights an enemy team automatically, turn order determined by Speed
- Type effectiveness, weather effects, and passive abilities all influence the outcome

**Progression**
- Survive 13 increasingly difficult rounds: Gym Leaders → Elite Four → Champion
- Lose HP when your team faints; reach 0 HP and it's Game Over



## Features

- **140+ Pokémon**
- **120+ unique abilities** — on-hit effects, weather setters, entry hazards, healing, stat buffs, and more
- **Weather system** — Sun, Rain and Sandstorm with visual particle effects and stat modifiers
- **Full Pokédex** — browsable in-game with search, type filter, and tier filter
- **Hall of Fame** — records your best winning teams
- **Persistent settings** — resolution, windowed/fullscreen toggle, and separate volume sliders for music, SFX, and weather sounds
- **Scene-persistent overlay** — Settings and Pokédex panels accessible from every scene without reloading



## Built With

- **Unity**
- **C#**
- **TextMeshPro**
